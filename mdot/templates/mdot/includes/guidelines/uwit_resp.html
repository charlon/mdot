UW-IT Services & Responsibilities

  • Creation of Application ID and Assignment of App Development Manager

    UW-IT shall create a single application ID and assign a person to fill the role of Primary Developer (specified above).

  • Audit

    UW-IT may perform audits to ensure that accounts are up to date and applications are in good standing. We are also responsible to audit any apps if we have received any formal complaints and/or requests from Apple/Google.

  • App Suspension and/or Removal

    UW-IT reserves the right to suspend or remove apps in the store that violate any of the terms and conditions described above.

  • Support

    UW-IT provides minimal technical support in the form of technical advice/guidance and knowledge expertise. No support is provided for application development.
