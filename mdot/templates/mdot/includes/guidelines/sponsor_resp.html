Sponsor responsibilities

  • UW Terms and Conditions

    The Sponsor will be responsible for adhering to the conditions set out in the UW Terms and Conditions.

  • Apple and/or Google Play Terms and Conditions

    The Sponsor will be responsible for adhering to the conditions set out in the Apple and/or Google Play terms and conditions.

  • Usability (User Experience)

    The UW is committed to providing good user experience for our audiences. For assistance, free UX consultations are available with UW-IT's AXDD design team.

  • Accessibility

    It is UW policy that all software is accessible. For assistance, UW-IT's Accessible Technology Services (ATS) team offers accessiblity consultations/reviews.

  • UW Branding

    If applicable, the Sponsor will ensure that the App adheres to the use of UW logo and brand guidelines.

  • Privacy Practices

    The Sponsor will be responsible for adhering to the UW Online Privacy Statement.

  • Information Security

    The Sponsor will be responsible for adhering to/and implementing information security policies and complying with relevant laws and regulations.

  • External Vendors & Developers

    If the Sponsor is working with an External Vendor or Developer, ehe Sponsor will be responsible for ensuring the vendor/developer is in compliance with UW policies regarding data security and privacy as well as any necessary agreements to that regard are in place. Procurement Services may be able to provide guidance.

  • Due Diligence

    The Sponsor agrees to undertake the due diligence to adhere to the UW values and demonstrate compliance with all UW policies mentioned and/or may otherwise be relevant.
