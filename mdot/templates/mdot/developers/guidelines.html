Publishing Roles

Publishing an Application (App) through the UW Mobile Developer Programs, will require adhering to the followingroles and responsibilities:

  • Application Sponsor

    The Sponsor is a Director or above (or equivalent) at the unit level within the University of Washington.

  • Primary Developer

    The Developer will be responsible for code maintenance and app distribution.

  • UW-IT

    University of Washington, Information Technology (UW-IT) will be responsible for granting access to the various Application developer programs.
