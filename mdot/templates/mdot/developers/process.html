UW Developer Store Publishing Process

So, you have developed a native app and want to publish it under the UW Developer Store? Here is what you do next.

  1. Step 1

    Confirm that your app meets the UW Developer Store Publishing Guidelines.

  2. Step 2

    Submit your application to request access to the UW Developer Store.
    • Designate an appropriate Primary Developer who will be granted access to the UW Developer Program.
    • Designate an appropriate App Sponsor who will be responsible for adhering to the conditions set in the UW App Developer Terms and Conditions.

  3. Step 3

    The designated App Sponsor will receive an email asking them to agree to the UW App Developer Terms and Conditions.

  4. Step 4

    The UW-IT Mobile Team will process the request and grant the designated Primary Developer access to the UW Developer Program.
